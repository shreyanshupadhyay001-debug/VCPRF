About the Institution

Bridging Research,
Governance & Implementation

Veritas Centre for Policy Research Foundation (VCPRF) is an emerging policy think tank focused on bridging research, governance, and implementation. We engage with institutions, governments, and scholars to generate evidence-based insights that inform consequential decisions.

Our work spans constitutional law, administrative governance, regulatory policy, and institutional design — grounded in rigorous methodology and aimed at practical policy impact.

Active Research Initiative
Live Archive

A Structured Digital Legal-Policy Archive

A dynamic digital archive documenting the constitutional, legal, and administrative evolution of the Sixth Schedule in India.

The Sixth Schedule creates a unique framework of autonomous governance for tribal areas in Northeast India. This archive documents, contextualises, and makes accessible its complex constitutional history and legal precedents.
