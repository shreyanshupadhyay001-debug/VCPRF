Publication Categories

Our publications span three formats, each designed for a specific audience and purpose within the policy research ecosystem.

Policy Papers

Policy Papers

Concise analytical papers addressing specific governance and policy questions. Aimed at practitioners, administrators, and legislators requiring structured, evidence-based guidance on contemporary issues.

Typical length: 3,000 – 8,000 words

Available
Research Reports

Research Reports

Comprehensive reports on thematic areas of governance, institutional design, and regulatory reform. These in-depth studies provide sustained analysis suitable for academic and institutional reference.

Typical length: 15,000 – 40,000 words
