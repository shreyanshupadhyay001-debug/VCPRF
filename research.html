Research & Policy Work

Independent, evidence-based inquiry at the intersection of constitutional law, administrative governance, and institutional design.

Core Mandate

What We Do

VCPRF's research activities are structured around five core institutional objectives.

01

Government EOIs & Tenders

Participating in government Expressions of Interest and competitive tenders to deliver evidence-based policy solutions at institutional scale. VCPRF brings research rigour to public sector engagements.

02

Independent Policy Research

Undertaking rigorous, independent research across constitutional law, administrative governance, and regulatory design with methodological integrity and scholarly depth.

03

Journals & Policy Reports

Publishing peer-reviewed journals, working papers, and policy reports that contribute to the academic and practitioner literature on governance and public administration.

04

Seminars, Fellowships & Competitions

Organising academic events, research fellowships, and policy competitions to nurture the next generation of governance scholars and practitioners in India.

05

Advisory Services

Providing strategic advisory and institutional consulting to governments, corporations, and civil society organisations navigating complex policy and governance challenges.

How We Work

Research Methodology

VCPRF follows an interdisciplinary research methodology that combines doctrinal legal analysis, empirical fieldwork, comparative governance studies, and stakeholder consultations.

Our research process is structured to ensure intellectual rigour, practical applicability, and policy relevance. All outputs undergo internal peer review before publication or submission.

1

Problem Identification & Scoping

Identifying policy questions with governance significance through stakeholder mapping and literature review.

2

Multi-Method Analysis

Doctrinal, empirical, and comparative analysis across legal, administrative, and policy domains.

3

Peer Review & Refinement

Internal and external review processes ensure quality, accuracy, and policy relevance of all outputs.

4

Dissemination & Engagement

Publication, events, and direct stakeholder engagement to translate research into governance action.
