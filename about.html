Our Identity

Who We Are

Veritas Centre for Policy Research Foundation (VCPRF) is an independent, non-partisan policy think tank established to undertake rigorous research at the intersection of law, governance, and public administration.

We draw our name and purpose from the Latin root veritas — truth — reflecting our commitment to evidence-based, impartial inquiry. Our work is grounded in intellectual honesty and directed toward practical, implementable policy outcomes.

VCPRF operates at the confluence of constitutional law, administrative governance, regulatory policy, and institutional design. We collaborate with governments, academic institutions, corporate entities, and civil society to produce knowledge that matters.

Thematic Focus

Areas of Work

VCPRF's research and advisory work concentrates on the following thematic domains.

Constitutional Law & Governance

Research on constitutional frameworks, federalism, special provisions, and the legal architecture of Indian governance.

Administrative & Regulatory Policy

Analysis of administrative structures, regulatory design, and the reform of public institutions for effective service delivery.

Tribal Governance & Special Areas

In-depth study of Fifth and Sixth Schedule areas, tribal self-governance, and the administrative challenges of special constitutional regions.

Rigorous in method. Impartial in analysis. Consequential in impact.
